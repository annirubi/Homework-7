OUR PROJECTS

ARCHITECTURAL PROJECTS

  1. ArchiPark Studio team loves architecture of any size and purpose: from private houses to administrative, public buildings and shopping centers, From residential quarter to small architectural forms and landscaping. Creating architecture, we are not just doing a project and all its stages, we are thinking about scenarios of human life, who will live, work, relax and study there. Creating architecture is a multilayer process, it begins with a concept and then thins with each stage. We consider the path from general to particular and analytical approach to the process of creating forms as reference points of our design method.
  2. Modern interior design is the solution to problems. The tasks of the budget, functionality, beauty and much more. In creating interior design, we use an analytical approach, it allows us to design both private housing and offices, cafes, restaurants, bars, galleries, museums, etc. We proceed from the tasks that the client and the interior space pose before us. We are thinking about how to rationally use the implementation budget and create a unique design for a specific task.
  3. Items in the interior should be aesthetic and comfortable. Each item of your personal space should bring joy. Furniture should be beautiful in itself, it is a finished work, a product of the thought process of a particular designer, constructor, assembler, etc. We think about every item that we create, think about ergonomics, functionality, aesthetics, we love our every piece of furniture. Our design objects can be purchased at our branded showrooms located in Minsk.
  4. At a certain stage of life, a person comes to the decision to build a house for himself. All over the world, the process of designing individual housing takes a long period of time and is quite expensive. If you do not have the time and budget to create an individual project at home, we give you the opportunity to buy a finished project from us. Each of the projects that we offer was developed by our company. This is not a typical Polish or Lithuanian project, these are houses already adapted to the materials and standards of the CIS countries. All you need is to take the finished project, tie the house to the plot and build it. You can choose the constructive system of the house, layout, facade materials, roofing, and we will help you adapt the project to your budget.
